Observing a robot peer's failures facilitates students' classroom learning
Liuqing Chen†*, Yu Cai*, Yuyang Fang, Ziqi Yang, Duowei Xia, Jiaxiang You, Shuhong Xiao, Yaxuan Song, Lingwei Zhan, Juanjuan Chen, Lingyun Sun†
借鉴"有效失败"理论，通过机器人主动示范错误促进学生知识学习、缓解课堂压力。Leveraging productive failure theory, a robot peer demonstrating mistakes facilitates student learning and reduces classroom pressure.
Science Robotics ↗

根据有效失败（PF）理论，在问题解决过程中经历失败可以增强学生在后续教学中的知识习得。然而，让学生直接面对超出其当前能力的问题可能会对其技能、先验知识和情绪健康造成负担。为此，我们设计了一种社交机器人辅助教学活动，让学生观察机器人的失败尝试，在无需亲身经历失败的情况下提供类似有效失败的准备效应。我们在一所初中开展了两项课堂研究。研究1（N=135）在八年级数学课上比较了三种教学方法——观察机器人失败、个人问题解决失败和直接教学。观察机器人失败组在概念理解方面进步最大，且社交压力最低。后续研究2（N=110）在两周机器人适应期后进一步验证了该方法的有效性。研究结果表明，观察机器人失败对知识习得的效果可与甚至优于亲身经历失败，凸显了社交机器人作为 STEM 教育同伴的价值。 According to productive failure (PF) theory, experiencing failure during problem-solving can enhance students' knowledge acquisition in subsequent instruction. However, challenging students with problems beyond their current capabilities may strain their skills, prior knowledge, and emotional well-being. To address this, we designed a social robot-assisted teaching activity in which students observed a robot's unsuccessful problem-solving attempts, offering a PF-like preparatory effect without requiring direct failure. We conducted two classroom-based studies in a middle school setting. In study 1 (N=135), students in the robot failure condition showed the greatest gains in conceptual understanding and reported lower social pressure. Follow-up study 2 (N=110) further validated the method's effectiveness after a 2-week adaptation phase. Our findings suggest that observing the robot's failure has a comparable, or even greater, effect on knowledge acquisition than experiencing failure firsthand, underscoring the value of social robots as peers in STEM education.

Measuring Human Perception of Airflow for Natural Motion Simulation in Virtual Reality
Yu Cai, Sanyi Jin, Zihan Chen, Daiwei Yang, Han Tu, Preben Hansen, Lingyun Sun, Liuqing Chen†
提出了使用气流阵列营造 VR 环境中二维运动体验的方法，并首次定量建模了气流刺激与 VR 运动感觉的映射关系。Proposed airflow arrays for 2D motion experiences in VR and established the first quantitative model mapping airflow stimuli to VR motion perception.
IEEE TVCG ↗

气流被认为是诱导虚拟现实中自我运动错觉（vection）和减轻晕动症的有效方法。然而，虚拟运动与被感知为与之一致的气流之间的定量关系尚未被充分探索。本研究通过三个实验填补了这一空白。实验1通过跨模态匹配任务建立了虚拟运动速度与感知一致气流速度之间的强线性相关。实验2引入"气流梯度"概念模拟曲线运动的身体感觉，发现运动半径与左右气流速度差呈线性关系，角速度呈近似二次方模式。实验3基于上述发现开发了动态气流方案，结果表明动态气流能进一步减轻晕动症、增强临场感，提供更自然一致的虚拟运动体验。 Airflow is recognized as an effective method for inducing vection and reducing motion sickness in VR. However, the quantitative relationship between virtual motion and consistent airflow has not been fully explored. This study conducted three experiments. Experiment 1 established a strong linear correlation between virtual motion speed and perceived consistent airflow speed. Experiment 2 introduced the "Airflow Gradient" concept for curvilinear motion simulation, revealing linear and near-quadratic relationships. Experiment 3 developed a dynamic airflow scheme that further reduces motion sickness, enhances presence, and provides a more natural virtual motion experience.

Supporting text entry in virtual reality with large language models
Liuqing Chen†, Yu Cai, Ruyue Wang, Shixian Ding, Yilin Tang, Preben Hansen, Lingyun Sun
首次提出了虚拟现实环境下基于大语言模型的预测性文本输入方法。First work proposing LLM-based predictive text entry for virtual reality environments.
IEEE VR ↗

虚拟现实中的文本输入在效率和任务负荷方面面临挑战。本研究利用大语言模型（LLM）在上下文感知和文本预测方面的能力，通过减少用户手动按键来提升输入效率。提出了三种 LLM 辅助文本输入方法——简化拼写、内容预测和关键词到句子生成——分别对应英文文本在单词、语法结构和句子层面的上下文可预测性。在 Oculus VR 原型上的用户实验表明，三种方法分别减少了 16.4%、49.9%、43.7% 的手动按键，效率提升 21.4%、74.0%、76.3%，同时显著降低了身体、心理和时间负荷。 Text entry in VR often faces challenges in efficiency and task loads. This study leverages LLM capabilities in context perception and text prediction to enhance efficiency by reducing manual keystrokes. Three LLM-assisted methods — Simplified Spelling, Content Prediction, and Keyword-to-Sentence Generation — are introduced at word, grammatical, and sentence levels. User experiments on an Oculus VR prototype demonstrate 16.4%, 49.9%, 43.7% reductions in keystrokes, translating to 21.4%, 74.0%, 76.3% efficiency gains respectively, while significantly reducing physical, mental, and temporal loads.

Enhancing VR Shopping With Dynamic Space Re-Arrangements: Towards Contextualized Information Displays and Improved Locomotion
Liuqing Chen†, Yu Cai, Ruyue Wang, Preben Hansen, Tingting Zhou, Yankun Zhen, Lingyun Sun
研究了虚拟现实购物应用中的空间导航方式和场景设计问题。Investigated spatial navigation and scene design in VR shopping applications.
IDETC/CIE ↗

传统 VR 购物应用通常依赖静态的购物空间设计，需要频繁传送。通过对资深网购用户的探索性案例研究，我们发现了交互性、效率、晕动症和产品展示等方面的问题。为此，我们与专业设计师合作提出了购物空间动态重排的概念，用户可以动态重排购物空间及其中的商品，利用百货商店、货架、房间等空间形式适配探索、比较和搭配等不同购物意图。被试内比较实验表明，该方案在参与度、晕动症、任务效率、产品感知和整体用户体验方面均有显著提升。 Traditional VR shopping apps rely on static space designs requiring frequent teleportation. Through an exploratory study with experienced online shoppers, we identified issues with interactivity, efficiency, motion sickness, and product presentation. We proposed dynamic rearrangement of shopping space, enabling users to rearrange spaces and products using department store, shelf, and room forms for different shopping intentions. A within-subjects experiment demonstrated significant improvements in involvement, motion sickness, efficiency, product perception, and overall user experience.

Take the Dog to the Park: Quadruped Robot for Joint Attention Training with Autistic Children in Naturalistic Settings
Yuyang Fang, Yi Hu, Jiayu Teng, Yu Cai, Jiayang Liu, Feifan Xia, Yilin Tang, Liuqing Chen†
CHI ↗

针对自闭症儿童的机器人支持共同注意力（JA）干预已显示出良好效果，但大多局限于室内固定机器人，限制了技能泛化和更广泛的发展效益。我们提出了一种利用四足机器狗作为同伴在室内外环境进行 JA 训练的干预方案。机器狗引导儿童关注环境中的分布式目标并发起 JA 任务。一项为期四周的探索性研究表明，六名自闭症儿童的 JA 表现有所提升，并显示出向日常社交交流迁移的迹象。还观察到了运动模仿和新型社交互动等自发行为，表明其具有更广泛的发展潜力。 Robot-supported interventions for joint attention (JA) in autistic children have shown encouraging outcomes, yet most remain confined to stationary robots in indoor settings. We introduce an intervention employing a quadruped robot dog as a peer-like partner for JA training across indoor and outdoor environments. A four-week study with six autistic children demonstrated improvements in JA performance and indications of transfer to daily social communication. Spontaneous behaviors such as motor imitation and novel social interactions also emerged, suggesting potential for broader developmental gains.

Emoeden: Applying Generative Artificial Intelligence to Emotional Learning for Children with High-Function Autism
Yilin Tang, Liuqing Chen†, Ziyu Chen, Wenkai Chen, Yu Cai, Yao Du, Fan Yang, Lingyun Sun
CHI ↗

高功能自闭症（HFA）儿童常常面临情绪识别与表达的困难，导致情绪困扰和社交障碍。先前研究中为 HFA 儿童开发的对话代理由于无法动态生成个性化和情境化内容，学习效果有限。我们集成大语言模型和文生图模型开发了 EmoEden 工具。在为期 22 天的研究中，六名 HFA 儿童参与体验，EmoEden 有效吸引了儿童参与并提升了其情绪识别和表达能力。同时，我们识别了生成式 AI 应用于 HFA 儿童情绪学习的优势与潜在风险。 Children with high-functioning autism (HFA) often face challenges in emotional recognition and expression. We integrated large language models and text-to-image models to develop EmoEden, a tool supporting HFA children in emotional learning. Over a 22-day study involving six HFA children, EmoEden effectively engaged children and improved their emotional recognition and expression abilities. We also identified advantages and potential risks of applying generative AI to assist HFA children.

CHI 2023CCF-A
Lasershoes: Low-Cost Ground Surface Detection Using Laser Speckle Imaging
Zihan Yan, Yuxiaotong Lin, Guanyun Wang†, Yu Cai, Peng Cao, Haipeng Mi, Yang Zhang
CHI ↗

地面表面通常经过精心设计，具有不同纹理以适应人类环境的功能需求，因而包含丰富的上下文信息。地面检测可支持活动识别、移动健康和情境感知计算等广泛应用。我们提出了 LaserShoes，一种基于激光散斑成像的纹理感知系统，可改装到鞋子上。系统捕获地面产生的散斑图案视频，并使用 ResNet-18 模型实现实时推理。评估结果验证了该方法的可行性。 Ground surfaces contain rich context information for smart wearables. We present LaserShoes, a texture-sensing system using laser speckle imaging that can be retrofitted to shoes. The system captures speckle pattern videos on ground surfaces and uses a ResNet-18 model for real-time inference. Evaluation results verified the feasibility of this approach for ground surface detection.
